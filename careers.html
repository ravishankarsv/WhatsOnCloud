Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 1991 as Hughes Software Systems, a research and development subsidiary of Hughes Networks Systems, Aricent has transformed industries. In 1992, we pioneered the first ground-to-air phone call. We designed, developed and delivered the first-ever commercial mobile high-definition solution in 2008. Our engineers created some of the world.s first Femtocell, WiMAX and LTE solutions. Working across domains and technologies gives our employees the innovative insights needed to visualize new possibilities and transform concepts from ideas into everyday technology.

World Class Opportunities

Aricent works to solve the world.s biggest challenges and push the limits of what.s possible. Our engineers work with best-in-class technology and collaborate on the systems of tomorrow. We.ve created more than 125 licensable enabling software rights and software frameworks, and our software innovations perform on more than 500 million mobile phones. Our clients include:

  • 9 of the top 10 network equipment manufacturers
  • 8 of the top 10 device makers
  • 7 of the top 10 service providers
  • 6 of the top 10 semiconductor chip manufacturers

We have some of the most extensive product engineering skills and expertise on the planet. We.re always seeking better approaches, ideas and outcomes to improve the world through technology.

Culture and Values

Aricent is a global company, but we pride ourselves in maintaining a cohesive culture across all of our locations. Our culture begins with five core values:

  • Forward looking
  • Constantly learning
  • Unified in diversity
  • Passionate about excellence
  • Integrity in everything we do

      Learning and Career Development

      We continually invest in our employees and establish a consistent learning experience across teams and locations. Our employees engage with new technologies and work across disciplines through our global mobility and job rotation programs. We take a holistic approach to developing talent through learning opportunities and rewarding career experiences.

      Work Environment

      Our work environment encourages open communication, a flat hierarchy and respect for new ideas. We take a flexible approach to support the highly collaborative nature of our work and the personal needs of our employees. We recognize that our employees have lives beyond work, and supporting a healthy work.life balance builds a more sustainable workplace. Our people are our strongest assets, and our comprehensive compensation and benefits packages reflect that conviction. We recognize employees with competitive rewards based on contribution to our clients, our business and our people.
